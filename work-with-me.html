IIT Bombay + SigmaStream LLC · 2020–2021 · Master's Thesis

Regime-aware forecasting framework for non-stationary time series — published as Indian patent

The problem

Time series from industrial signals (oil drilling, cardiac monitors) shift regime mid-stream — standard ARMA models assume stationarity and fail when the data's statistical properties change.

The approach

Wavelet transforms convert time-series segments to images → pretrained CNN extracts features → clustering discovers regimes automatically → per-regime ARMA models forecast. No manual regime labelling required.
